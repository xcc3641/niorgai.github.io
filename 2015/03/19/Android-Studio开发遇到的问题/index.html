Android Studio开发遇到的问题

  1. Gradle运行失败：

    Error:Execution failed for task ':app:dexDebug'.
com.android.ide.common.internal.LoggedErrorException: Failed to run command:
      /Users/qiu/Library/Android/sdk/build-tools/21.1.1/dx --dex --no-optimize --output /Users/qiu/svn/mam/trunk/app/build/intermediates/dex/debug --input-list=/Users/qiu/svn/mam/trunk/app/build/intermediates/tmp/dex/debug/inputList.txt
      Error Code:
          2
      Output:
  UNEXPECTED TOP-LEVEL EXCEPTION:
  com.android.dex.DexException: Multiple dex files define Landroid/support/v4/accessibilityservice/AccessibilityServiceInfoCompatIcs;
      at com.android.dx.merge.DexMerger.readSortableTypes(DexMerger.java:596)
      at com.android.dx.merge.DexMerger.getSortedTypes(DexMerger.java:554)
      at com.android.dx.merge.DexMerger.mergeClassDefs(DexMerger.java:535)
      at com.android.dx.merge.DexMerger.mergeDexes(DexMerger.java:171)
      at com.android.dx.merge.DexMerger.merge(DexMerger.java:189)
      at com.android.dx.command.dexer.Main.mergeLibraryDexBuffers(Main.java:454)
      at com.android.dx.command.dexer.Main.runMonoDex(Main.java:302)
      at com.android.dx.command.dexer.Main.run(Main.java:245)
      at com.android.dx.command.dexer.Main.main(Main.java:214)
      at com.android.dx.command.Main.main(Main.java:106)

    此时删除app/build目录下的全部文件,然后重新gradle即可。

  2. Gradle没有自动下载dependence包。

    解决方法：

    1. 检查gradle环境。
      1. 在工程目录下依次运行gradle clean , gradle assemble

  3. Executor Singleton not started

    升级一下gradle到最新版本,然后在project的build.gradle中修改gradle版本即可.

  4. java finished with non-zero exit value 2

    这是libs里面有包重复了,找出来删掉就好了
